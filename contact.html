Have Questions?

How quickly can we get started?
After our initial strategy call, most clients are onboarded and campaigns are live within 7–14 business days. We move fast because every day without great marketing is opportunity lost.
Do you require long-term contracts?
We start with a 1-month minimum engagement, then continue month-to-month. We believe in earning your business every single month.
What makes Adplix different from other agencies?
Three things: radical transparency (you see everything we do and why), senior talent on every account (no juniors managing your budget), and a results-first model where we're genuinely invested in your growth metrics — not just deliverables.
How do you measure and report results?
You get a real-time performance dashboard, weekly progress updates, and a comprehensive monthly strategy review. We track revenue-first metrics — ROAS, CAC, LTV, and pipeline growth — not vanity metrics like impressions.
What industries do you specialize in?
We specialize in professional services. Our frameworks are built to help service-based businesses grow their client base, build authority, and generate consistent leads.
